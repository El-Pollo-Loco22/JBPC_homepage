How I Beat Cancer

Little did I know that my frequent night time trips to the bathroom, weak urine flow, and waning sexual performance were early signs of a more serious health issue. I attributed these symptoms to aging, never imagining that cancer was quietly brewing within my prostate. When my PSA levels rose to a 4, I was rushed off to a urologist. Despite a series of tests and biopsies, my initial diagnosis was benign. I was put on watchful waiting.


Two years later, I was diagnosed with prostate cancer. My PSA levels had skyrocketed to 13. I had three tumors filling my prostate with Gleason scores of 6, 6 and 7. The doctor’s recommendation of surgery or radiation were presented as the only viable choices. He said active surveillance was probably not a good option because the cancer seemed aggressive. He also said the only downside to those treatment options would be the loss of making semen.

Something felt off. Despite the fear, I couldn't shake the feeling that I wasn't getting the full story. I delved into researching surgery and radiation, only to discover a multitude of potential side effects and uncertain outcomes. I sought out various healthcare practitioners for second opinions, asking countless questions. It became clear that there was a different path, a more holistic approach that aligned with my values. The concept of eliminating cancer, while keeping my body in tact felt like a risk worth taking. So, I chose active surveillance.

Those conversations along with extensive research led me to
a protocol that I would use while in active surveillance. It was a combination of alternative medicine, proven medical treatments,
a healthy diet and proper exercise. This approach not only halted the progression of my cancer but also led to its complete eradication.
