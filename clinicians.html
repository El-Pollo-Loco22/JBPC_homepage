Why me

This work comes from experience—not theory

I'm Jacob Williams—a filmmaker and prostate cancer survivor.

I've sat where your patients sit: trying to understand what's real, what matters, and what to do next. That experience shaped how I approach this work.

Patients aren't just choosing between treatments—they're trying to make sense of a complex and often unclear path. For many, that complexity comes from understanding how different approaches to care work together.

Clear information patients understand and can act on.
