The Jacob C. Williams Wellness Foundation

Jacob Beats Prostate Cancer is more than just a personal journey—it’s a movement. This
initiative is dedicated to raising awareness about alternative treatments, proactive health
measures, and self-advocacy. Through a groundbreaking documentary and an ongoing online
series, we provide valuable insights into nutrition, fitness, and holistic healing, equipping
individuals with the knowledge to make informed health decisions.

The Jacob C. Williams Wellness Foundation
‍

As part of his commitment to giving back, Jacob established 
The Jacob C. Williams Wellness Foundation, Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to:

  • 1) Education: Providing accessible resources on cancer prevention, holistic wellness, and alternative healing.
  • 2) Patient Support & Mentorship: Guiding men diagnosed with prostate cancer through mentorship programs and self-advocacy tools.
  • 3) Research & Funding: Supporting studies and initiatives that explore integrative and alternative health solutions.
The Documentary & Online Educational Series

The upcoming documentary & "Jacob Beats Prostate Cancer" tells Jacob’s compelling story of survival without surgery, radiation, or chemotherapy. It features expert interviews and evidence-based insights into holistic cancer care.

Alongside the film, the Jacob Beats Prostate Cancer Online Series will offer a broader range of expert-led discussions on integrative medicine, nutrition, fitness, and spiritual wellness.

How We Sustain & Expand Our Mission

To continue providing life-changing resources, the foundation utilizes multiple revenue streams:

  • 1) Grants & Sponsorships: Partnering with health-focused corporations, wellness brands, and philanthropic organizations.
    2) Donor Support & Fundraising: Direct contributions from individuals who believe in holistic health and cancer prevention.
    3) Merchandise & Digital Products: Offering wellness guides, meal plans, and online courses to empower individuals with actionable knowledge.
    4) Workshops & Speaking Engagements: Hosting live events and seminars featuring leading experts in alternative health.
